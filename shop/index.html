ABOUT ARTWORKS

These original works are painted in layers of oil, acrylic or mixed media fine artists paints on professional quality canvases. The artist likes to experiment and to invent different techniques, shapes and textures, using all kinds of tools and materials such as sand, paper, cement, fabrics, small pieces of wood, etc. Adina is not in search of a personal style, since her own vision is the one that naturally confers uniqueness to all her artworks!

Each artwork purchased through this site will be individually signed by the artist Adina Cicort. Paintings are signed in the right corner at the bottom and on the back. They come unframed, painted on the edges so they can be hanged without a frame, if desired.

Please email us for a detailed description of any artwork that captured your attention!
