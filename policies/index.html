SHIPPING

The prices include shipping and handling fees only for USA shipments. Prices do not include sales tax 8.25% for Texas buyers and purchases. For shipping outside USA, please contact me for international shipping rates and any additional information. After receiving your order, you will receive a confirmation message. Please allow 1 to 3 business days for processing your order. We ship through major carrier such us USPS, FedEx and UPS. We do not ship to P.O.Box addresses! "Shipping not required" is an option only available for on site sales, when artworks are picked up from the artist's studio, exhibition, fare or any other on site selling location.


RETURN

Your satisfaction is one of our primary goals at Adinasart.com
We are glad to accept returns within 5 days from the confirmed date of receipt for any reason. To return the item(s) you must receive prior authorization, simply call us at 972 922 4217. Please note, that returns cannot be accepted without prior authorization.
Buyer must repack with the original packaging and return the product(s) and any additional accessories; buyer pays return shipping. Upon receipt of item(s) in original condition, we will refund the total amount paid minus initial shipping and handling charges, within 7 days via PayPal.


DAMAGED SHIPMENT

Please inspect your package as soon as you receive it. If you notice any damage, please call us at 972 922 4217 to report the damage and do not use the product. If your product arrived damaged, please make sure to contact us within 1 business day. In this case, keep all packaging (boxes, shipping labels, packing material, etc...), as the shipper might come to inspect the package.


ONLINE PAYMENTS

All orders made from adinasart.com will be processed by PayPal. ADINASART.COM does not directly accept or process credit card orders. When you place an order using a credit card, you can rest assured knowing that your credit card number has been transmitted safely and securely. ADINASART.COM never has direct access to your credit card number. Instead, it is processed by PAYPAL using a secure server.
If you have any questions at all, please don't hesitate to contact us at 972 922 4217 or email adina@adinasart.com Thank you!
