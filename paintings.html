Biography

Adina Cicort is a visual artist, publicist and entrepreneur living in Dallas, Texas.  Adina was born in 1978 in Bucharest, Romania. Although she discovered the artist within since childhood, due to the difficulties of living during Ceausescu’s Communist Regime and after the 1989 Romanian Revolution, she chose to embrace a career in economic sciences. The artist was revealed later on, while living in the United States.

Her childhood memories are reflected in her entire artistic work.

”My mother’s stories, songs and drawings are the seed of my inspiration, along with the memories from the time spent with my grandparents at the country side, in Ialomita at Helis- the ancient capital of King Dromichaetes - and in the mysterious Carpathian Mountains- the most beautiful time of my life. My beloved grandparents were simple people, talented crafters and with an extraordinary spirit and understanding of Life, God and Nature. Everything I remember about them inspires me.”

Adina’s art is defined by color. She sees the world through light and color. Her paintings are vivid, colorful, energetic…

Adina’s abstract paintings are philisophical ideas, believes and feelings expressed through color on canvas. The passion for symbolism, history and philosophy, is a powerful source of inspiration.
